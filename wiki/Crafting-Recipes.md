# Crafting Recipes (Player Guide)

This page documents every recipe a player needs to know for crafting or producing items added or changed by the Misc Datapack (Minecraft 1.21.1). It covers vanilla recipe changes, new campfire/cooking recipes, recovery recipes for slabs and planks, custom craftable items like chainmail armor and spawners, and notes on mod compatibility.

## Vanilla recipe changes

Logs-to-planks: most log types now yield fewer planks when crafted normally.

- 1 log → 2 planks for: Acacia, Birch, Cherry, Crimson, Dark Oak, Jungle, Mangrove, Oak, Spruce, Warped
- Bamboo → 1 Bamboo Plank

Tip: pre-processing (see "Stripped Oak Log to Planks") can still be more efficient for some logs.

Charcoal: vanilla smelting-to-charcoal recipes have been disabled. Produce charcoal using the Campfire Charcoal feature (drop logs on a lit campfire — see Campfire section).

Torch: crafting a torch via campfire is supported — place a Stick on a lit campfire (takes 30 seconds).

Other minor vanilla additions:
- Item Frame, Iron Trapdoor, and Log→Stick utility recipes have been added/adjusted for consistency.

## Campfire & cooking

Torch Arrow
- Cook an Arrow on a campfire to obtain a Torch Arrow. Cook time: 30 s.

Rotten Flesh → Leather
- Cook Rotten Flesh on a campfire to convert it into Leather. Cook time: 4 minutes.

Charcoal (Campfire method)
- Drop a log on a lit campfire and wait for the pack's Charcoal feature to process it (this replaces the disabled smelting recipes).

-## Recovery recipes (player-friendly crafting patterns)

- Place a single log in any crafting slot to get 2 planks.
- Or, to get 4 planks: place a log in the world, right-click it with an axe to strip it, then right-click the stripped log again with the axe to obtain 4 planks.

Slabs → Planks / Blocks (vertical stack)
- Put two identical slabs stacked vertically in your 2×2 crafting grid (or any 2 vertical slots) to recover 1 full plank/block.

Slabs → Planks / Blocks (2×2 table)
- Place 4 slabs in a 2×2 square in a crafting table to recover 2 full planks/blocks.

Planks → Sticks
- Place 2 identical planks stacked vertically (in a single column) to craft 2 Sticks.

## Custom craftable items

Chainmail Armor
- Surround an Iron armor piece (helmet/chestplate/leggings/boots) with Iron Bars in a 3×3 crafting table to create the corresponding Chainmail piece. Each produced chainmail piece includes a small armor bonus and comes pre-enchanted.

Horse Saddle
- A saddle can be crafted using Leather and a Golden Apple in the pattern shown in-game (standard shaped recipe used by the pack).

Spawner (advanced)
- The datapack provides a special crafting option to convert resources into a Monster Spawner (high-cost shaped recipe using Iron Bars + Nether Star). It also provides egg → spawner/egg conversion recipes for convenience.

Torch Arrow & Rotten Flesh
- See Campfire & cooking section for campfire cooking recipes.

Misc utility recipes
- Many conversion helpers are included (slabs→planks, planks→sticks, stripped logs→planks, remove MI torch, etc.) to make building and mod compatibility smoother for players.

## Mod compatibility

Modern Industrialization (MI)
- The pack includes MI compatibility entries that disable MI's torch and lignite torch recipes when MI is present, and adds a utility recipe to remove MI torches if needed.

Railcraft
- Compatibility recipes for Railcraft items (tank wall / siding) are present for integration when Railcraft is installed.

Snad
- If Snad is installed, the pack provides crafting for `Snad` using Suspicious Sand + Bone Meal.
